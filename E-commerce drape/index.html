Our Story

Fashion that respects its origins

DRAPE was born in 2019 when founder Priya Natarajan walked through the weaving villages of Kanchipuram and realised that India's most extraordinary textile traditions were slowly disappearing — not for lack of skill, but for lack of a modern platform.

She set out to change that. DRAPE became a bridge between master artisans and customers who craved clothing with a story — garments that felt as good as they looked, and lasted longer than a season.

Today, DRAPE partners with over 200 weavers, embroiderers, and block-printers across Tamil Nadu, Rajasthan, West Bengal, and Gujarat — ensuring fair wages, safe working conditions, and creative dignity for every craftsperson we work with.

What We Stand For

Our Values

♻

Sustainable by Default

We use GOTS-certified organic cotton, natural dyes, and recycled packaging. Our supply chain is audited annually for environmental impact.

✂

Uncompromising Craft

Every piece passes through a 12-point quality check before leaving our atelier. We reject fast fashion shortcuts and stand behind every stitch.

🇮🇳

Made in India

100% of our production happens in India. We pay artisans 40% above the industry standard and provide healthcare benefits to all workshop partners.

⚖

Radical Transparency

We publish our cost breakdowns publicly. When you pay ₹2,499 for a dress, you know exactly where every rupee goes — from cotton to courier.

✦

Inclusive Sizing

Our range runs from XS to 4XL across all categories. We photograph every product on multiple body types because fashion belongs to everyone.

🌱

Climate Commitment

We're carbon-neutral since 2022. Our shipping partner offsets every delivery, and 1% of all revenue goes to Indian reforestation projects.

How We Got Here

Our Journey

2019

The Beginning

DRAPE launches with 12 products, a single Chennai pop-up store, and a mission to bring artisan fashion online. First 500 orders shipped in 3 months.

2020

Going Digital

Pandemic accelerates our shift online. We onboard 40 new artisan partners and launch the Kantha Stitch collection — which sells out in 72 hours.

2021

Seed Funding

₹3Cr seed round from Blume Ventures and angels. Expand to Bengaluru and Mumbai. Cross 10,000 customers and launch Kids & Accessories lines.

2022

Carbon Neutral

Achieve carbon-neutral status. Launch our Repair & Return programme — customers can send worn DRAPE pieces back for upcycling instead of landfill.

2023

Series A

₹18Cr Series A. Launch DRAPE for Business (corporate gifting), expand artisan network to 200+, and open flagship stores in Chennai and Bengaluru.

2024

₹10Cr Revenue

Cross ₹10Cr annual revenue. Named one of Forbes India's 30 Under 30 brands. Launch AI Stylist feature and same-day delivery in 6 metro cities.
